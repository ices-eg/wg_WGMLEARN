## Terms of Reference

The Terms of Reference define the scope and remit of the working group, and are revisited and changed for each three-year period.
A copy of the current terms of reference can be found in the [docs/](docs/) directory.  In brief:

### ToR a

Develop a list of relevant topics and tasks for the application of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML), with emphasis on unmet challenges specific to the marine domain and potential benefits. Integrate the output from Workshop on Applying AI in ICES (WKAAII) and link to relevant ICES working groups, research groups, and public datasets.

### ToR b

Establish contact with other ICES working groups to explore collaboration opportunities and to chart research and knowledge needs. Support the development of aspects of AI at the ICES strategic level.

### ToR c

Based on the outcomes of ToR a and b, organize a series of 5–10 events to target important challenges. These can take the form of tutorials, hackathons, workshops, or conference sessions ,co-organized with relevant ICES working groups or other entities.
